About us

                What is an Online Examination System? Online Examination System is a technology-driven way to simplify examination activities like defining exam patterns with question 
                banks, defining exam timer, objective/ subjective question sections, conducting exams using the computer or mobile devices in a paperless manner.
                An online exam (also called eExam) is a great way of conducting tests and other important exams with help of the internet. An online exam needs a device capable 
                of accessing the internet such as a computer or a smartphone. This can be either done at an examination center or at home.
